Events

TECH TALK [ T ]

Rules

  • Student should form a team of 3
  • The paper format should be in IEEE format
  • The topic should be related to engineering field
  • The PPT should be in the given KEC template only
  • Student can present up to a maximum of 7 mins including question and answers
  • Three members should share equal amount of points
  • Students can have a maximum of 10 slides in their PPT

IDEATHON [ T ]

Rules

  • Student should form a team of 3
  • Students should present their project ideas through ppt and poster
  • Presentation should be above 6 minutes excluding viva session
  • Size of the poster should be 297 x 420 mm[A3]
  • A poster should contain minimum of one image, one graph and one table
  • If poster contains creativity will be appreciated

SCARP TO PROTOTYPE [ T ]

Rules

  • Student should form a team of 3
  • The first round will be conducted as a quiz (Mechanical & Electrical topics).
  • Shortlisted teams will design a wooden or metal prototype using only scrap materials.
  • The PPT should be in the given KEC template only
  • The winning team will be selected based on the innovation and presentation of their prototype.
  • A panel of judges will evaluate the prototypes created by each team

CODING AND DEBUGGING [ T ]

Rules

  • Individual Participation
  • Round one Will be in Quizizz platform
  • Students should find output for the given question or else debug the program
  • Students who get shortlisted in first round will move to second round
  • Round two will be in hacker rank website
  • Students should write the code for the given problem statements

CONNECTION [ NT ]

Rules

  • Team should contain maximum 3 members
  • Time for answering 30 secs and its a buzzer round
  • Those who scores max in the set will proceed to the next round
  • No of rounds will be based on participants

FLIP FLAP [ NT ]

Rules

  • Student should form a team of 3
  • Time allotment will be 3 minutes for every team
  • There will be a bowl containing number of sheets
  • Each sheet contain different movie names
  • One member should tap the balloon using hands only
  • Other two members have to pick a sheet from bowl and act
  • The one who tapping the balloon should find the word
